# Harm From Hexxy

April Fools mod

When Consideration is added to the intro/retro stack (just drawing consideration is safe, as long as it won't end up in the final pattern list), you will be attacked by several hexxies, attempting to beat you to death.

Not sure if the hexxies are their own kind of entity, or just invisible zombies with a mask or something. They will definitely have a chance to drop a hexxy mask, regardless of whether they're zombies.
